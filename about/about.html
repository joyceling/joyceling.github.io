About Joyce

Joyce Ling graduated from the University of Colorado at Boulder in May of 2017 with a BA in Molecular Cellular Developmental Biology.

After visiting her sister in San Antonio, TX for a few months, she started attending a coding bootcamp called Codeup , which teaches full-stack software development utilizing HTML, CSS, and jQuery for the front-end, MySQL for database management, and Java with a Spring framework for the back-end.

Currently, she is working part-time with Youth Code Jam, teaching K-12 youth about computer programming and coding.

She is also learning React, serverless technologies via AWS, and looking for further job opportunities to grow her skill-set and contribute to something bigger than herself.
